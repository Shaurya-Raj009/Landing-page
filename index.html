Courses
B.Tech

Bachelor of Technology, or B Tech is an undergraduate degree that focuses on technical and practical aspects of engineering and technology. The full form of B Tech is Bachelor of Technology. It is an important degree that can lead to many opportunities in the field of engineering and technology.

M.Tech

M Tech stands for Masters of Technology. It is a course that focuses on the advanced study of science and technology. The focus of the course is engineering and information technology. It is a two-year postgraduate programme with four semesters.

B.Arch

BArch course is a 5-year undergraduate program that focuses on the principles and practice of architecture. In the course, you'll learn about architectural design, construction techniques, building materials, structural systems, and more..
